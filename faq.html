FAQ

  • Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We got answers!

1. How long does the picnic last?

Picnic base price's are for 2 1/2 hours only. You can add additional hours to your picnic when selecting a package. Each additional hour is $50.

2. What if there's bad weather?
If the weather takes a turn for the worst and we need to postpone, we will allow you 2-3 days in advance to cancel and postpone the event date. (This option is only available twice).
3. Can I cancel?
If you need to cancel your event, you will need to cancel 10 days in advance in order to receive a full refund of your deposit. Anything after 10 days reservation deposit will not be refunded.
4. Can I pick any location?

We will email you different locations that we offer but if you have some place in mind, we can make it happen.

5. How much time in advance is needed to book?

We require at least 72-hour notice to get your picnic prepared. Please call us or send us an email if you would like to book a picnic within the 72-hour required notification window.

6. What if we damage something?

If there is any damage to our equipment, our team will be notified and the credit card on file will be charged.

In case you haven't found the answer to your question please feel free to contact us and our customer support will be happy to help you.
