FAQs

    Bad Weather

  • If the weather takes a turn for the worst and we need to postpone, we will allow you 3 months to pick another date.

    Cancellation

  • If you need to cancel your event, you will need to let us know 72-hours ahead of the event to receive a full refund.
  • If you need to cancel within the 72-hour window leading up to the event, we will refund you 50% of your purchase price.

    Location

  • We will email you different locations that we offer but if you have some place in mind, we can make it happen.
  • Extra fees will be applied if the location is outside of Miami.

    Damage

  • If there is any damage to our equipment, our team will be notified and the credit card on file will be charged.

    Booking in advance

  • We require a 72-hour notice to get your picnic prepared. Please call us or send us an email if you would like to book a picnic within the 72-hour required notification window.

    Extra guests

  • There is an extra charge of $45 per person.
