FAQs

    Bad Weather

  • If the weather takes a turn for the worst and we need to postpone, we will allow you 2-3 days in advance to cancel and postpone the event date. (This option is available only twice).

    Cancellation

  • If you need to cancel your event, you will need to let us know 10 days in advance. (Reservation deposit will not be refunded)

    Location

  • We will email you different locations that we offer but if you have some place in mind, we can make it happen.

    Booking in advance

  • We require a 20-day notice to get your picnic prepared. Please call us or send us an email if you would like to book a picnic within the 20-day required notification window.

    Reservation Deposit

  • There is a $125 Deposit For Reservation Date (non refundable) Remaining Balance due 10 days before picnic.
